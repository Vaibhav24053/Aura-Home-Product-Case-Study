02 — Market Research

Four Gaps.
Zero Competitors.

Before writing a single line of code, a market scan revealed four clear opportunities every existing player had missed — all four became core features.

GAP 1
No Free AI Room Visualizer for India
Modsy and Roomvo are US-centric, paid, and show Western furniture. Indian consumers need Indian aesthetics with Indian product links.
GAP 2
Vastu + Modern Design Has No Digital Home
Vastu consultation costs Rs.5,000+ per session. An AI that blends Vastu with contemporary aesthetics simply doesn't exist anywhere.
GAP 3
No Community for Real Budget Transformations
Instagram shows aspirational rooms with no context, creating paralysis. No platform lets real people share honest before/after numbers.
GAP 4
Design Personality Is Completely Unexplored
A quiz-based design archetype gives users a shareable framework — and creates a built-in viral acquisition loop at zero cost.

08 — Key Learnings

What This Build
Actually Taught Me

Four non-obvious lessons from a weekend build that shipped a full-stack AI product. Not theory — observed from doing.

01
Vibe coding is a real product skill
Using Replit AI Agent with structured prompts, a non-developer can ship a full-stack product in a weekend. The skill is knowing WHAT to build and WHY — the AI handles HOW.
02
India-specific = automatic differentiation
Vastu-Modern, INR pricing, and Indian store names cost zero extra dev time but created a product no competitor offers. Localisation is the cheapest moat.
03
The quiz is the best acquisition feature
Shareable personality results drive organic growth with zero paid spend. Turns a cold start into a warm first session.
04
Reliability beats free when it matters
Switching from Gemini's free tier to GPT-4o-mini was the right call. A working prototype is worth more than a broken one that runs on zero budget.
05
Experimenting across tools sharpens judgment
Also tested OpenAI Codex and Antigravity. Codex for precise code, Antigravity for rapid UI, Replit AI for end-to-end. The skill is knowing which to reach for when.

Prototype Disclaimer
⚠️ Prototype Disclaimer — Please Read

Generated room images may not accurately reflect your real space. This is a prototype — not a production-grade application. Using GPT-Image-1 means the AI interprets your room from a single photo and applies a style prompt. Results are directional and inspirational, not pixel-perfect.

To generate truly accurate, room-specific outputs would require training custom diffusion models on large datasets, significant ML engineering, and a dedicated team. That is a different product at a different scale.

The budget planner, Vastu recommendations, and community tools are functional prototypes built to validate ideas, not polished production features.

This is a case study and a learning exercise — proof that the concept is valid, the market gap is real, and the user need is clear.
